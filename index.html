About Our Company

Founded in 2015, we are China’s largest manufacturer exclusively dedicated to wooden boat paddles and oars. With a 4,000-square-meter facility and a monthly export capacity of 2 to 3 high-cube 40-foot containers, we combine industrial-scale production with artisanal craftsmanship to deliver premium paddles for the global marine market.

Our strength lies in bridging high-end quality with accessible pricing—offering a full spectrum of mid-range to high-end paddles that meet the rigorous demands of both leisure boaters and professional dealers. From wood selection to final finish, every paddle reflects our commitment to durability, ergonomic design, and consistency at scale.

Certificates & Compliance

Our company complies with the EU Deforestation Regulation (Regulation (EU) 2023/1115). We have established a due diligence system to ensure that all wood used in our paddle products is deforestation‑free, legally harvested, and fully traceable to its origin. We submit due diligence statements as required under the regulation and maintain all supporting documentation for verification.

Our EUDR compliance is supported by our [FSC/PEFC] Chain of Custody certification, which ensures the traceability and legality of our wood supply chain.

This certificate is issued by China Customs. It certifies that our factory meets the Chinese government’s requirements for exporting bamboo, wood and grass products, including facility hygiene, pest control measures, quality management system, and product traceability. This registration is a prerequisite for exporting wooden products to many countries including the EU, the US, and Japan.
