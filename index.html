About Our Company

Founded in 2015, we are China’s largest manufacturer exclusively dedicated to wooden boat paddles and oars. With a 4,000-square-meter facility and a monthly export capacity of 2 to 3 high-cube 40-foot containers, we combine industrial-scale production with artisanal craftsmanship to deliver premium paddles for the global marine market.

Our strength lies in bridging high-end quality with accessible pricing—offering a full spectrum of mid-range to high-end paddles that meet the rigorous demands of both leisure boaters and professional dealers. From wood selection to final finish, every paddle reflects our commitment to durability, ergonomic design, and consistency at scale.
