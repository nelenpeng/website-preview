Sawyer Story Kids Canoe Paddle with Animal Graphics Adventure Log and T-Grip for Small Hands

  • A beloved children’s paddle featuring charming animal graphics, designed to spark a love for paddling, nature, and fishing.
  • Includes an adventure log and ruler printed on the face to record a child’s first canoe trips and discoveries.
  • An ideal introductory paddle for children ages 2-10, creating a perfect keepsake of their early paddling experiences.
  • Features a 7-inch offset between the blade and shaft, an oval wood shaft, and a T-grip tailored for small hands.
  • Available in two lengths (38’ or 46’) and two blade graphics (Dragonfly or Otter) to suit young adventurers.
